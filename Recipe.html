Recipe of my favourite food

Why it is my favourite?

Biriyani is basicaly a mixed rice dish which can be prominently seen in Indian subcontinent. It consist of rice, meat, sometimes vegetables and egg. But being this my favourite dish is because we use Indian spices for the prepration in which it makes this dish extreamly flavourful and aromatic. Meat includes either chicken, goat, beef, lamb, prawn or fish which is also the prime ingredient with rice. The spices and seasonings used in biryani include ghee, pepper, cloves, cardamom, cinnamon, bay leaves, coriander, mint leaves, ginger, onions, tomatoes, green chilies and garlic. The premium varieties of biriyani include saffron also.

Chicken Biriyani

    Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Basmati rice
  • Ginger paste
  • Garlic paste
  • Chilly powder
  • Garam masala
  • Ghee
  • Curd
  • Salt
  • Mint leaves
  • All spices(Pepper, Cloves, Cardamom, Cinnamon, Bay leaves, Coriander)
  • Mint leaves
  • Turmeric powder

    How to cook

  1. Wash the basmati rice🍚 and soak it for sometime.
  2. Put the Chicken pieces🍗 into a medium large vessel and add ginger paste, garlic paste, a spoon🥄 of chilly powder, garam masala, turmeric powder, pepper powder, salt🧂, some mint leaves and some curd.
  3. Mix all the ingredients with a spoon🥄, add sufficient water to cook the chicken🍗 and place it into the flame🔥.
  4. Take another vessel to cook the rice🍚.
  5. Put the soaked Basmati rice🍚 along with all spices, salt🧂 and water, stir well and keep it in the flame to cook.
  6. Cook for approximately 10 minutes⌚ in high flame🔥 and another ten minutes⌚ in low flame.
  7. Switch of the flame🔥 and serve🍽 the delicious biriyani😋.
